Our Approach

How Vanyax works

A hybrid agency and platform model — we start with your specific challenge and build toward reusable, scalable infrastructure.

01 — UNDERSTAND 🌿

Ecology first, data second

We start with your field reality — what species, what terrain, what decisions need to be made. Data science without ecological context produces useless numbers.

02 — AUDIT 🔍

Map every data asset

Camera traps, satellite imagery, sensor networks, acoustic recordings, survey data. We identify what you have and exactly what intelligence can be extracted.

03 — BUILD ⚡

Production-grade pipelines

Reproducible, containerised ML pipelines validated against ecological ground truth — not just statistical benchmarks. Everything is documented and deployable.

04 — SCALE 📡

Platform subscriptions

Repeated solutions become platform modules — species APIs, habitat dashboards, deforestation alerts — so every new client benefits from the work done before.

The Person Behind Vanyax

Built by someone who understands the field

Vanyax exists because I kept seeing the same problem everywhere: extraordinary field data being collected by passionate, underfunded conservation teams — and almost none of it being analysed in a way that actually informs decisions.

After years building ML systems across computer vision, NLP, and geospatial analysis, I realised that the tools existed — the domain expertise to apply them to ecology was what was missing. Vanyax is the answer to that gap.
