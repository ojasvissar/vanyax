Who We Are

Built for
ecologists,
by data scientists

Conservation and wildlife agencies are drowning in data — camera traps, satellite feeds, sensor networks, field surveys — but lack the in-house capacity to turn it into intelligence.

Generic data science agencies don't understand NDVI indices, species distribution models, or acoustic biodiversity signatures. We do. That gap is where most projects fail. We bridge it.

2026

Geospatial Multimodal Yield Forecasting Transformer

Extended MMST-ViT for agriculture crop yield prediction by fusing satellite imagery with time-series weather signals. Added Grad-CAM attention visualisation to surface which spatial regions and temporal windows drive predictions. Exported to ONNX for optimised inference and benchmarked against baseline geospatial models.

PyTorch TorchGeo ViT ONNX Grad-CAM

Dec
2025

222-Year Climate Change Forecasting Pipeline

Modelled 222 years of Berkeley Earth geospatial temperature data. Benchmarked three regression models across RMSE, MAE, and R². Containerised full pipeline with Docker and delivered via reproducible Quarto report — projecting 2030 temperatures approximately 2°C above the 1951–1980 baseline.

Python SVR Random Forest Docker Quarto
↗
Nov
2025

Asteroid Hazard Classification — 958K NASA/JPL Records

Built a full ML pipeline on 958,524 NASA/JPL records. Applied SMOTENC for class imbalance, RFE and chi-squared feature selection, and benchmarked XGBoost vs Random Forest across F1, precision, and recall. Fully documented architecture for scientific reproducibility.

XGBoost Random Forest SMOTENC Feature Engineering

How We Work

The Vanyax process

01

Understand the ecology

We start with your field reality — what species, what terrain, what questions matter. Data science without ecological context is just numbers.

02

Audit your data

We map every data asset you have — camera traps, satellite feeds, sensor logs, surveys — and identify what can be activated with ML.

03

Build & validate

We build reproducible, containerised pipelines. Every model is validated against ecological ground truth, not just statistical benchmarks.

04

Deploy & hand over

We deploy tools your team can actually run. Every project ends with documentation, training, and an optional platform subscription.
