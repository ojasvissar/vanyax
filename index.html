The Problem

Conservation is drowning
in data — starved of insight

Wildlife agencies, NGOs, and research institutions collect extraordinary data — camera trap images, satellite feeds, sensor networks, acoustic recordings. Almost none of it becomes actionable intelligence.

The problem is not the data. It is the absence of data science capacity built specifically for ecological work. Generic firms don't understand the domain. In-house teams are too expensive. The gap is costing us species.

Our Approach

Ecology first,
data second

A hybrid agency and platform model — we start with your specific challenge and build toward reusable, scalable infrastructure that outlasts any single project.

01
Understand the field reality

We begin with your ecological context — species, terrain, decisions that need to be made. Data science without domain knowledge produces useless numbers.

02
Audit every data asset

Camera traps, satellite imagery, sensor networks, acoustic recordings, survey data. We map what you have and precisely what intelligence can be extracted.

03
Build production-grade pipelines

Reproducible, containerised ML pipelines validated against ecological ground truth — not just statistical benchmarks. Fully documented, fully deployable.

04
Scale as platform modules

Repeated solutions become platform features — species APIs, habitat dashboards, deforestation alerts — so every new client benefits from prior work.

The Person Behind Vanyax

Built by someone who
speaks ecology

Vanyax exists because I kept seeing the same problem everywhere: extraordinary field data collected by passionate, underfunded teams — and almost none of it being analysed in a way that informs real decisions.

After years building ML systems across computer vision, NLP, and geospatial analysis, I realised the tools existed — what was missing was the domain expertise to apply them to ecology. Vanyax closes that gap.
