The challenge

Conservation teams are
data-rich, insight-poor.

Field sensors generate terabytes of camera footage, acoustic recordings, and GPS traces — but most organizations lack the technical infrastructure to turn raw data into decisions. Fragmented pipelines, manual analysis, and outdated baselines leave critical windows for action unopened.

Vanyax exists to close that gap. We bring production-grade AI and data engineering to conservation — the same tools that power billion-dollar tech companies, built for the science and scale that biodiversity demands.

What we do

Four ways we help
conservation teams.

Species Monitoring

End-to-end ML pipelines from camera traps, acoustic sensors, and GPS collars. Species identification, individual re-ID, and real-time population dashboards — no manual data entry.

  • Computer vision
  • Acoustic ML
  • Real-time

Habitat Intelligence

Multispectral satellite imagery processed by deep learning to map land cover, deforestation pressure, and habitat connectivity. From patch to continental scale.

  • Remote sensing
  • Deep learning
  • GIS

Biodiversity Analytics

Audit-ready data pipelines, biodiversity index tracking, and compliance dashboards aligned with TNFD, CSRD, and the Kunming-Montreal Global Biodiversity Framework.

  • TNFD / CSRD
  • Dashboards
  • Data pipelines

Climate Risk Modeling

Species distribution models under CMIP6 scenarios. Identify refugia, migration corridors, and extinction hotspots — so conservation plans account for where the world is going, not where it was.

  • CMIP6
  • SDM
  • Scenario analysis

How we work

From raw data to
real decisions.

01

Discover

We map your data landscape — sensors, formats, storage, gaps. Understand the decisions you need to make and work backward to the systems that enable them.

02

Architect

Design the right pipeline for your context. We don't over-engineer. A field team of 12 needs different infrastructure than a government agency covering 40 million hectares.

03

Build

Develop ML models, ETL pipelines, and dashboards. Tight feedback loops with your scientists — models that your ecologists can interrogate and trust.

04

Deploy

Production-grade infrastructure with monitoring, alerting, and documentation. Built so your team can run it independently — we're not here to create dependency.

05

Evolve

As data accumulates, models improve. We set up retraining loops, evaluate drift, and quarterly check-ins to keep systems sharp as conditions change.
