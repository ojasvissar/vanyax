WHAT WE BUILD

01

Species Monitoring Pipelines

End-to-end data ingestion from camera traps, acoustic sensors, and GPS collars. ML classification, individual ID, and real-time dashboards.

02

Habitat Mapping

Multi-spectral satellite imagery processed by deep learning to map land cover, deforestation pressure, and habitat connectivity at continental scale.

03

Climate Risk Modeling

Species distribution models under CMIP6 climate scenarios. Identify refugia, migration corridors, and extinction risk hotspots for conservation planning.

04

Biodiversity Reporting

Automated, audit-ready reporting aligned with TNFD, CSRD, and the Kunming-Montreal Global Biodiversity Framework. Turn field data into compliance evidence.
